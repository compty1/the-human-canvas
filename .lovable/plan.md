

# LeCompte Portfolio - The Human Experience

## Vision
A bold, pop art-powered portfolio that captures your multidisciplinary creative work exploring the human experience. The design will feature halftone dots, comic book panels, vibrant clashing colors (magenta, cyan, electric yellow, black), bold hand-drawn-style typography, and dynamic panel layouts that mirror your distinctive artistic voice. ## Core Pages & Structure

### 1. **Home / Landing Experience**
A scrolling immersive intro featuring:
- Animated entrance with your LeCompte signature logo
- Hero section with rotating artwork pieces as backdrop
- Bold mission statement about exploring the human experience
- Quick-nav comic panels linking to major sections (Art, Projects, Writing, Support)
- "Currently Working On" ticker showing active projects

### 2. **Art Gallery**
- Masonry grid of your artwork with pop art frame effects
- Filter by style: colored digital, pencil sketches, mixed media
- Each piece opens in a detailed view with like button, description, and story behind it
- Halftone overlay effects on hover
- Your uploaded artwork will be featured prominently

### 3. **Tech Projects Hub**
Feature your three live projects with full case study pages:

**Live Projects:**
- **Notardex.com** - Virtual notebook platform
- **Solutiodex** - Community-driven solution search engine  
- **Zodaci.com** - Birth chart & astrology site

Each gets a dedicated page with: screenshots, problem/solution narrative, link to live site, tech stack badges, and a like/heart counter.

**In-Progress Projects (sample content):**
- **T1D Compass** - Type 1 diabetes management and community tool
- **Pulse Network** - Social change organizing platform
- **UX Lens** - Product/service experience review case studies

Each shows: project vision, progress status, concept mockups, and ways to sponsor/fund.

### 4. **Writing & Content**
Organized by category with demo content in key areas:
- **Philosophy & Metaphysics** - Essays and explorations
- **Narrative Stories** - Creative fiction and personal journeys
- **Cultural Commentary** - Journalism on art, change, society
- **UX Reviews** - Product and service experience breakdowns
- **Research & Informative** - News and educational pieces

Features: reading time estimates, topic tags, like buttons

### 5. **Skills & Expertise**
Visual skill showcase with pop art icons:
- Web Development & UX Design
- Content Creation & Journalism
- Visual Art & Illustration
- Research & Analysis

### 6. **Future Plans & Learning**
Interactive roadmap showing:
- Skills you want to learn (with progress indicators)
- **Fund My Learning** feature - visitors can contribute to specific courses/certifications
- Vision board for upcoming projects
- Areas of interest exploration goals

### 7. **Support & Funding Center**
Central hub for all contribution methods:
- **Donate** - One-time support with suggested amounts
- **Sponsor a Project** - Choose which in-progress project to fund
- **Fund a Class** - Contribute to specific learning goals
- Stripe integration for real payments
- Public thank-you wall for contributors (opt-in)

### 8. **About / Human Behind the Work**
Personal story page featuring:
- Your journey and passions (human experience, T1D, transformation stories)
- Areas of interest and what drives you
- Professional bio for client pitching
- Contact information and collaboration inquiry form

## Key Features

### Engagement System
- User accounts for liking projects and tracking engagement
- Like counters visible on all projects and artwork
- "Most Loved" highlights section

### Pop Art Design System
- Halftone dot patterns and Ben-Day dots
- Comic panel layouts with bold black outlines
- Color palette: magenta, cyan, electric yellow, orange, black, white
- Hand-drawn style typography for headers
- Speech bubble elements for testimonials/quotes
- Screen-print texture overlays

### Client-Pitch Ready
- Downloadable PDF media kit
- Clear service offerings section
- Past work examples with results
- Easy contact/inquiry form

### Real Backend (Supabase)
- User authentication for likes and donations
- Project and artwork database
- Payment processing via Stripe
- Contribution tracking and thank-you features
